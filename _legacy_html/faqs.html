FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What exactly is Streamline?

Streamline is a Cognitive Interaction Operating System that unifies messaging, voice, video, social, and workflow automation into one centralized platform. It helps businesses manage communication, collaboration, and operations without juggling multiple disconnected tools.

How is Streamline different from traditional communication tools?

Traditional tools focus on one channel (just messaging or just VoIP). Streamline connects every interaction  - across voice, SMS, video, social, and internal workflows - into one intelligent system powered by automation and cognitive logic. It’s not just communication. It’s operational infrastructure.

Who is Streamline built for?

Streamline supports:

  • Sales teams

  • Logistics & operations

  • Marketing departments

  • Customer support teams

  • Financial management teams

  • Enterprise organizations

  • Growing SMBs

If your business relies on communication to operate - Streamline is built for you.

Is this a CRM?

No - but it integrates seamlessly with your CRM. Streamline enhances your CRM by powering real-time conversations, automated workflows, messaging intelligence, and cross-channel interaction tracking.

What communication channels does Streamline support?

Streamline supports:

  • SMS / MMS

  • SIP & VoIP Calling

  • Video communication

  • Social messaging

  • Fax

  • Internal team messaging

  • File & asset sharing

All inside one interface.

Do my customers need to download an app?

No. Your customers communicate through standard channels (SMS, voice, social, etc.). No app installation required.

Can I keep my existing phone numbers?

Yes. You can port existing numbers or provision new ones directly through Streamline.

Does Streamline support VoIP and SIP?

Yes. Streamline supports SIP/VoIP infrastructure and integrates with carrier-grade systems.

Can departments share the same number?

Yes. You can route, assign, and automate conversations across departments with workflow logic and role-based access.

What is the Cognitive Interaction Operating System?

It’s Streamline’s intelligence layer. It connects communication events to workflows, automation rules, decision trees, and operational triggers - turning conversations into automated actions.

  • Marketing follow-ups

Yes. AI can be integrated for:

  • Smart routing

  • Interaction insights

  • Predictive workflow triggers

  • Data classification

Can I build custom modules?

Yes. With Developer Access and APIs, you can build and extend custom modules on top of the platform.

Is Streamline secure?

Yes. Streamline is built with enterprise-grade security, encryption protocols, and controlled access permissions.

Is this carrier-grade infrastructure?

Yes. Streamline operates on reliable, scalable infrastructure designed for high-volume business communication.

Does Streamline support role-based permissions?

Yes. Admins can define user roles, access levels, and department-based permissions.

By consolidating tools, automating repetitive tasks, and centralizing communication, teams reduce manual work and increase operational speed.

Yes. Bulk messaging, routing logic, and automation workflows allow for emergency and high-priority communications.
